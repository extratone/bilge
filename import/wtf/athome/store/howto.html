How to Use the Store

Purchasing your Compaq products online is simple....and secure.

Whether you're a first-time PC buyer, or an enthusiast seeking the latest in technology, we've made it easy to purchase the computer and options that are right for you. Just follow these easy steps and review the Terms and Conditions or call us toll free at 1-800-888-0220 and a trained sales representative will assist you in completing your transaction. Thank you for choosing Compaq.

A Step-by-Step Guide to Buying Online at the Compaq Consumer Products Store.

  1. Select a product
    Chose from our selection of Compaq systems, options, accessories, and upgrades.

  2. Configure
    Configuration is available on select Presario systems. Chose a configuration option from the drop down list where available. All listed options are compatible with the base system.

  3. Put your product in the Shopping Cart
    Now that you've decided what you want, you're ready to buy it.
    Click on the "Continue" or "Buy Now" button to place the selected item in the shopping cart.

  4. Purchase the Product Online
    You can complete the purchase on-line, or by phone, mail or fax.
    • If all of the product information is correct, click "Place Order"
    • If you wish to change the quantity, click on "Recalculate" after making your change
    • If you wish to continue shopping to add more items to the shopping cart, click on "Keep Shopping"
    • You may cancel your transaction by clicking on "Cancel" and call our toll free number, 1-800-888-0220 to complete your order.
    Remember, prices shown in the Shopping Cart do not include sales tax or shipping and handling charges (if applicable). These charges will be automatically added to your total order after you complete the order information.

  5. Select a Shipping Method
    Chose Ground, Second Day, or Overnight. We ship promptly based on product availability (2 business days for Compaq Options and Accessories, 5 to 7 business days for PC's and monitors via second-day delivery, 4 to 6 business days via next day delivery).

  6. Select an Order Method
    Chose to place a secure online order, or to process your order via the telephone, fax, or by mail. For simple, safe, and secure on-line processing, select the "Secure Internet Transaction" option. You will receive an advisory message about security. Click on "Yes" to continue. (For more information on the security of online shopping, see our Internet Security section in this help guide).

  7. Enter Shipping and Billing Information
    Enter the appropriate shipping and billing information. Red triangles indicate required information.

  8. Confirm Your Order
    Once your online order has been successfully placed, you will be presented with an online receipt which will include your order reference number. If you selected an alternative order method (phone, mail or fax) you will be presented with a worksheet containing instructions for completing your order.
