Microsoft NT Enterprise Solutions -- Account to be announced
Microsoft NT is becoming a platform of choice for deploying mission-critical applications. This account will detail the design, sizing, and implementation of Micrsoft NT throughout its organization.

Novell Enterprise Solutions -- Qualcomm Inc.
Qualcomm Inc. will discuss the challenges encountered, as well as the obstacles they overcame, while designing and implementing a very large Novell IntranetWare LAN/WAN environment. The speaker will relate "tips and tricks" he learned and applied for the following areas:
Implementing a fault tolerant design for disk storage, network connectivity, and NDS
NDS design and troubleshooting: "Real World vs. Theory"
Designing optimum performance to accommodate a 2,500 user single-server platform
Solving the challenges of a 200+ GB single server: storage, backup, and disaster recovery
