To ensure that Compaq ASEs have the knowledge and expertise to perform at their best with Compaq platforms, periodic maintenance training is required by Compaq. The amount of maintenance training required throughout a year depends solely on Compaq product announcements, and whether Compaq determines that the new product training requires additional technical or presales training. ASEs are required to complete all these requirements to maintain their ASE accreditation.

The ASE is expected to complete the requirements within the amount of time specified by Compaq. This grace period may vary depending upon the requirement. Additionally, renewing ASEs are required to submit an ASE Application to Compaq regardless of whether there has been any maintenance training required during the previous year. Prior to their renewal date, ASEs will be sent a letter reminding them to submit an application. Failure to comply with the maintenance requirements of the ASE Program within the specified period of time will result in deaccreditation of the ASE.
