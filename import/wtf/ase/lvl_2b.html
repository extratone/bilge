To receive this accreditation, the Compaq Associate ASE candidate must demonstrate proficiency in knowledge, planning, installation, and configuration of Compaq hardware platforms. This is accomplished by passing three required Compaq exams: (1) Compaq Systems Technologies Test, (2) Compaq Integration & Performance Test-based on a specific operating system platform which is chosen by the Associate ASE candidate, and (3) Compaq Systems Management Test. Once the Associate ASE has successfully completed these tests, the candidate must submit an application to Compaq (See Chart 1). Accreditation is not automatic. A candidate must submit an application.
