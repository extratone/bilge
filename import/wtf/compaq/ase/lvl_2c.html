Tests required for the Compaq Accredited Systems Engineer Accreditations are offered at Sylvan Prometric (formerly known as Drake). Sylvan offers professional computer-based certification through a network of Authorized Sylvan Testing Centers throughout the US and Canada.

Preparing for ASE Tests

To assist ASE candidates in passing the tests required by the ASE Program, Compaq provides the following support:

1. Compaq Training

The highest recommended method of preparing for the Compaq ASE tests is by attending Compaq training. ASE Training information can be found in the Compaq Training Information Catalog. Also available are the list of the courses available and the schedule and registration information.

2. Compaq ASE Self-Assessment Guides

Compaq ASE Self-Assessment Guides provide topics and test information to help ASE candidates determine whether they have the requisite knowledge required to successfully pass the ASE Program tests. ASE candidates should be careful to review the Self-Assessment Guide specific to the test they are taking and, if applicable, the matching operating system platform of choice. These Self-Assessment Guides are also available via fax from Compaq PaqFax at (800) 345-1518. See the Compaq Training Information Catalog to obtain the document number for the appropriate ASE Self-Assessment Guide.
