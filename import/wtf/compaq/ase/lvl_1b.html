Becoming a Compaq ASE establishes a partnership between the ASE and Compaq. Upon accreditation as an ASE, the Systems Engineer automatically begins receiving essential tools and support, some of which are not available to System Engineers who are not ASEs. Additionally, as a reward for the hard work and efforts of becoming a Compaq ASE, Compaq provides many benefits to these individuals such as discounts and incentives for training and engineering events, product discounts, free subscriptions, etc. Benefits provided as part of the Compaq ASE Program are geared towards helping both the ASE as well as the employer. Following is a description of some of the benefits provided as part of the ASE Program.

Information and Support Tools Valued in Excess of $1500

(Available to ASEs and Associate ASEs)
The amount of information available to Compaq ASEs and Associate ASEs is endless. All ASEs automatically receive, at no charge, access to every technical tool and subscription available from Compaq. This ensures that along with their training, Compaq ASEs are equipped with the latest information, support tools, and software to successfully plan, install, troubleshoot, integrate and optimize Compaq solutions. Upon accreditation, ASEs receive a Welcome Kit to help them understand and fully utilize these resources from Compaq. After that, ASEs receive monthly subscription mailings from Compaq to ensure they have the latest information and tools available.

Once accredited, Compaq ASEs and Associate ASEs have toll-free access to an exclusive Compaq ASE Forum.The purpose of this forum is to enable ASEs to share information and leverage the expertise of other Compaq ASEs from around the world. Additionally, ASEs may dial into Compaq's technical databases 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, free of charge. ASEs also receive a quarterly ASE Newsletter containing information about upcoming events and the ASE Program.

(Available to ASEs)
Because of their extensive training on Compaq hardware and operating system integration, Compaq ASEs receive a free subscription to Compaq SmartStart -- the intelligent CD-based process that integrates hardware configurations and software installations into a single, streamlined process. Additionally, ASEs are privileged to free, priority access to Compaq technical support reducing hold times to a minimum.

Training

(Available to ASEs and Associate ASEs)
To ensure that Compaq ASEs are properly trained and maintain the expertise to perform as expected, periodic training is provided as well as private, non-disclosure events where ASEs can learn about upcoming Compaq products and technologies. Annually, ASEs are invited to the Compaq Systems Engineering conference where they attend break-outs and have a chance to speak directly with the top experts at Compaq. Priority registration, discounts, and incentives are provided to ASEs for most events.

Other Benefits

(Available to Associate ASEs and ASEs)
Advertising materials are provided to ASEs to gain them immediate recognition as a member of a worldwide team of highly trained Compaq Systems Engineers. These materials include use of the ASE logo for stationery and business cards, ASE brochures, and ASE catalog items including clothing and business accessories.

(Available to ASEs)
In return for their effort in becoming a Compaq ASE, ASEs receive special discounts on select Compaq products through a Compaq ASE Product Purchase Plan.
