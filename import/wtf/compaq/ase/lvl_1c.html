There are two levels of ASE accreditation available from Compaq.

(1) Compaq Associate ASE
Associate ASEs complete a curriculum that provides extensive training on the integration and configuration of Compaq hardware. To demonstrate their abilities as a Compaq Associate ASE, candidates must pass a strenuous series of Compaq tests.

(2) Compaq ASE
The Compaq ASE is considered an advanced level accreditation of the ASE Program. ASEs have successfully demonstrated their knowledge and expertise in the integration of Compaq hardware and a specified operating system. In addition to completing the rigorous training and requirements of the Associate ASE level, Compaq ASEs must also acquire and maintain the matching certification of a Compaq approved operating system vendor.
