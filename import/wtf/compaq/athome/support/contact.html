Compaq Support Forum
    The Compaq Support Forum is a web based message board where you can post your question, view other customer posts, and receive a reply from a Support Engineer. The Compaq Support Forum is available at http://forum.compaq.com.

    Compaq Technical Support Center
    1-800-OK-COMPAQ, 1-800-652-6672 U.S.
    Free technical support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, during the warranty period. After-warranty support is available for a fee of $19.95 1 (U.S.) per incident.

    1-900-733-HELP, 1-900-733-4357 U.S.
    After-warranty support is also available through a pay-per-call telephone service 2 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Calls will be billed through the user's telephone service at a rate of $2 1 per minute, not to exceed $20 1 (U.S.) per incident.
