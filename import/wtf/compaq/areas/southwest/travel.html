HOTEL & TRAVEL INFORMATION



THE OMNI LOS ANGELES HOTEL
Reservation Assistance: 1-800-520-2685
Reference Compaq Access Spring'97 Event
Address: 930 Wilshire Blvd, Los Angeles, CA


Hotel Location Information
Located in the heart of downtown Los Angeles financial center and downtown business district. Across the street from the Seventh Street Market and the Citicorp Plaza.

Driving Directions
Take the San Diego Freeway 405N to East Santa Monica Freeway to 110 North Harbor Freeway. Exit on 9th Street. At second street light turn left on Figueroa. Again at second street light, turn left on 7th Street. The Omni Hotel is on the right side.

Air Transportation
The Omni Hotel is 22 miles from the Los Angeles International Airport. Supershuttle cost per person one way is approximately $12.50. Taxi cost is approximately $24.00 flat rate plus a $2.50 surcharge.
