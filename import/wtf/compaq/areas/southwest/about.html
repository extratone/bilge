CONFERENCE HIGHLIGHTS


The Conference
The conference program brings together industry-leading vendors and key technologies providing access in the enterprise through:
Compaq Partner Solutions & Breakout Sessions
High-Availability Solution Presentations
The Partner Pavilion
Keynote Speech -- Build or Buy: Evolving Enterprise Integration Models
The Evolution of the High-Performance Workstation Presentation
Industry Leading Account Case Studies
The Access Lab: Hands-on Access to Compaq Partner Solutions


Compaq Partner Solutions & Breakout Sessions
Attend sessions by Compaq, Novell, Microsoft, Netscape, and Oracle on topics including: Internet/Intranet, NetPC, DataMart, Messaging, Management, Storage Solutions, and more.

High-Availability Solution Presentations
Attend two presentations focused on design of highly available systems platforms. One session will present product solutions by Compaq and Microsoft, including Microsoft cluster software. The other session will present product solutions by Compaq, Novell, & Vinca, including information on Novell clustering technologies.

The Partner Pavilion
Outside the sessions, you'll be able to view new technologies and innovative approaches needed to make global access a reality. The Partner Pavilion will give you access to information from our partners including Novell, Microsoft, Oracle, Netscape, Intel, SAP, Cheyenne, Seagate, Adobe, Raptor, Vinca, various Compaq services and Compaq integrators and resellers.

Keynote Speech -- Build or Buy: Evolving Enterprise Integration Models
Presented by Jim Schraith, Vice President & General Manager of North America for Compaq. Gain insight into the industry dynamics of this decade that can drive you to successful identification and implementation of key information system technologies.

The Evolution of the High-Performance Workstation Presentation
Explore the driving benefits of Intel-based workstations vs. proprietary RISC alternatives. Presented by Thomas G. Copeland, Ph.D., Director Workstation Research, IDC.

Industry-Leading Account Case Studies
Learn from successful account implementations of Compaq partner solutions as two industry- leading accounts share their information system design goals and implementations.

The Access Lab: Hands-on Access to Compaq Partner Solutions
For those who want a technical hands-on experience, Access Spring'97 provides a hands-on lab area. Hands-on access to new Compaq high-end servers, workstations, networking products, desktops, and portables. In addition, gain access to solution stations where you can build systems using Compaq hardware and Novell, Microsoft, Netscape, & Oracle software products. Twelve lab stations will be configured to offer broad access to many technologies.

Special Event of "Fun and Entertainment" -- Microsoft Sponsor Night
Microsoft and Compaq invite all conference guests to a special evening of food and entertainment during Access Spring'97. After the work day is over, you'll be able to spend "quality time" with new and old friends as Microsoft sponsors the party hosted by The Laughing Stock comedy group. You are sure to walk away with a smile.
