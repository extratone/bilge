Big Boy ‘Blast’

I spent far too much of last Wednesday pacing in front of and screaming at my television. I did not intend to watch the entirety of the Zuckerberg-Bezos-Cook-Pichai hearing, but I basically did, and it wore me the fuck out. I thought I’d try to make some use of all that time, so here I am… posting. I only have a few things to say, and I’ve already said the majority of it on Twitter.

Firstly, I took the opportunity to get one more test of Loom in and recorded myself using my least favorite piece of software in history to go over the pre-published copy of his statement. You’re welcome.

The spectacle of it all was bizarre in so many ways — the most original of these being the fact that it took place over virtual conference software, of course. To be honest, it was really gross. Jeff Bezos looked dead and vulnerable, Mark Zuckerberg looked like he was locked in a shed, Tim Cook looked fucking ancient, and Sundar Pichai appeared disturbingly and unwaveringly impeccable.

As in Zuckerberg’s first solo televised hearing, two years ago, the Congresspeople made an absolute fool of themselves — especially the white ones.
